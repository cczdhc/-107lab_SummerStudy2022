Henan Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Processing

         Henan Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Processing won the approval from the Science and Technology Department of Henan Province and was established in 2017 at the support of Henan University. It was formerly known as Henan University Laboratory of Intelligent Technology and System. In 2001, 2004 and 2007, it consistently ranked as a university-level key laboratory. In 2009, it was rated as the Open Laboratory of Key-Disciplines in Henan Province. In 2012, it was approved to be the Key Laboratory Base of Universities in Henan Province. The laboratory is located in Jinming campus of Henan University. At present, it covers an area of 1500 square metres, possessing large instruments such as high-performance computing platform, cloud computing platform, deep learning computing platform and integrated remote sensing data analysis and processing system. The total value of the instruments and equipment over 800 pieces is nearly 16 million yuan.

         There are 38 researchers in the laboratory, 35 with senior titles and 36 doctoral qualifications. Two researchers have got picked for the New Century Talents Project Level-‖. One has won the Henan Province Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars. Three people are supported by the Innovation Talent Fund in Henan Province. 8 are Henan Young Core Instructor. There are more than 80 postgraduates participating in collaborative researches. It is a scientific research team with reasonable structure, pioneering spirit and continuous progress.

         With the guidance of the national strategic demand and the policy of serving the local economy and to meet the needs of the social and economic development in our province, based on the government sponsored researches and scientific research projects consigned by enterprises in recent years, the laboratory is supported by the fundamental theory of big data and generic technology. It puts emphasis on the application of big data and exploits the demand of the industry. The three main research directions are big data theory and technology, remote sensing data analysis and processing, big data analysis and processing of vehicle network.

         n the past five years, the laboratory has carried out scientific and technology researches on the three directions and achieved considerable success. More than 300 academic papers have been published, including 200 in SCI and EI. More than 90 projects have been undertaken, including 4 China High-Resolution Earth Observation System(CHEOS) projects, 1 project of National Key Technology R&D Program, 1 sub project of National High-tech R&D Program of China (863 Program) and 27 projects supported by NSFC. In addition, the R&D achievements of the laboratory have received 15 awards for all kinds, including 4 of the Henan scientific and technological advanced awards. The team has obtained 26 patents and 13 software copyrights.

         The laboratory attaches importance to the cooperation with various research institutes at home and abroad. In China, the lab works with 27 groups or teams including the CNSA Center for Spaceborne Remote Sensing Demonstration, the Institute of Remote Sensing and Digital Earth (RADI) under the CAS, the Institute of Electrics under the CAS, the Administration of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense of Henan Province and China Electronics Technology Group Corporation. They send postgraduates and tutors and participate in CHEOS and major programs of remote sensing data. The successful cooperation with Technology Center of CHEOS, SEC under Ministry of Environmental Protection, Satellite Surveying and Mapping Application Center of NASG and Shenhua Group not only has greatly promoted the popularity of Henan University and Henan Province in the space information application field but also sets up a good image through outstanding contributions. Meanwhile, the lab has established research partnerships with University of Michigan, Wayne State University, University of Miami, University of Waterloo and other well-known universities or scientific research institutions. This long-term and stable cooperation has laid the foundation for a sound development of the laboratory.

         With the conditions going perfect and favorable, the laboratory will focus on the extension research of big data and carry out the application research. It will also begin study on Internet of things, cloud computing and deep learning and strives to be a national research platform. The laboratory has launched a series of software tools and products in the big data field. The scientific researches and application are the leading level in our country and reach the advanced level in the world.
