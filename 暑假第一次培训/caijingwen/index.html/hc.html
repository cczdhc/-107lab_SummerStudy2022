Henan International Joint Laboratory for Collaborative Technologies for Vehicle Networking

         Established in 2016, the International Joint Laboratory of Collaborative Technologies for Vehicle Networking in Henan Province has established long-term strategic partnerships with internationally renowned research institutes such as the University of Michigan, Wayne State University, Canada, Waterloo University of Canada, and Huawei Global R&D Center of Huawei. With the aim of serving the "two-class" construction, the laboratory has made every effort to establish the research direction and high-level international research team for the collaborative technology of smart transportation and vehicle networking. Smart traffic involves complex technologies such as artificial intelligence, collaborative communications, collaborative control, and distributed computing, which is a complex system engineering. It is crucial to the theoretical research and application development of its key technologies, and it is also the core link of smart city construction.

         At present, there are 23 scientific researchers in the laboratory, including 3 professors, 16 associate professors and 19 doctoral degrees. Committed to the intelligentization of automobiles and the theoretical innovation and industrial application of the connected network, closely following the national development strategy of the China Manufacturing 2025 ICV, the laboratory plays a crucial role in talent training and discipline construction in the area of ICV related to perception, communication, networking, control, computing, collaboration, decision-making. There are four major research directions in the laboratory, namely, the research on the vehicle networking collaboration system architecture, the research on the vehicle networking collaborative cognitive model, the construction of the vehicle networking evaluation and evaluation platform, and the construction of the vehicle networking application demonstration project.

         The laboratory undertook more than 20 scientific research topics such as the National Major Science and Technology Project, the National Natural Science Foundation, International Cooperation Projects, and Provincial Scientific and Technological Research Projects, and jointly established the “Henan University-Chery Automotive Collaborative Innovation Base”, and “Henan University- Aerospace Star Collaborative Innovation R&D Center”, “Henan University - Yixun Compass R & D Center” and other production, research and cooperation agencies. The laboratory attaches great importance to international exchanges and cooperation, actively participates in international flagship conferences such as IEEE Globecom, IEEE ICC, and IEEE VTC, and publishes the latest research results in international top journals such as IEEE Communications Magazine, IEEE Vehicular Technology Magazine, and IEEE Transaction on Vehicular Technology. It regularly selects outstanding scientific researchers to conduct joint research in North America, invites internationally renowned experts and scholars to come to the laboratory for guidance and exchanges, and actively builds first-class international laboratories.
