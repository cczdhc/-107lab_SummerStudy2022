Postdoctoral fellows Recruitment for School of Computer and Information Engineering Henan University

Our School

      The school has a first level doctoral degree in Software Engineering discipline, a secondary level doctorate degree in Remote Sensing Information Science and Technology discipline, 3 first level master’s degree in Computer Science and Technology discipline, Software Engineering discipline and Control Science and Engineering discipline, 5 undergraduate disciplines in Computer Science and Technology, Data Science and Big Data Technology, Network Engineering, Information Security and Automation. Computer Science and Technology is provincial key level discipline and provincial characteristic discipline, and Control Science and Engineering is provincial key discipline. There are 4 provincial science and technology innovation platforms including Henan Engineering Laboratory of Spatial Information Processing, Henan International Joint Laboratory of Vehicle Networking Technology, Henan Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Processing and Henan Intelligent Technology and Applied Engineering Technology Research Center. This school is building Henan Institute of Space-Time Big Data Applied Technology with the School of Environmental Planning. There are school’s key research institutions such as the Institute of Data and Knowledge Engineering, the Institute of Image Processing and Pattern Recognition and the Advanced Control and Intelligent Information Processing Institute here.

      The school now seeks to appoint a number of highly engaged scientists including Postdoctoral Fellows.

Minimum Criteria

      Applicants for the Postdoctoral fellowship should have a PhD in Computer Sciences, Software Engineering, Control Science or related fields. They must demonstrate initiative, be hard working, versatile and productive. Priority will be given to applicants having strong publication records. Candidates with good organizational and English communication skills are encouraged to apply:

The Role:

  • 1) Conduct research programmes assigned by the supervisor. The successful applicants will have demonstrated potential to establish independent research programmes.
  • 2) Apply for research funding from National research funding schemes or related industry.
  • 3) Participate in the teaching and supervision of research students
  • 4) Contribute to team effectiveness, leadership/management and administration.
  • 5) Actively participate in the preparation of publications, patents and others

Salary Scale:

      Appointments will be commensurate with qualifications and experience, and will be made on the appropriate point of the University salary scale.

      Additional allowance will be provided depending on the performance on the research outcomes (papers, patents and secured funding) according to the incentive pay policy.

Application Procedure

      Applications should contain a letter of personal statement along with a detailed CV containing a list of publications and be submitted by email to Mrs. Zheng (zhshsh@henu.edu.cn). The shortlisted candidates with required expertise will be interviewed shortly.
